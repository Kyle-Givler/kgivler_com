In the beginning, Tim create the HyperText Markup Language. The internet was without form and void, and text was upon the face of the monitor and the Hands of Tim were moving over the face of the keyboard. And time said, Let there be links; and there were links. And time saw that the links were good; and Tim seperated the links for the text. Tim called the links Anchors, and the text he called Other Stuff. And the whole thing together was the first Web Page.
